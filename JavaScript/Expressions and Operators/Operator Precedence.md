Grouping    --->    ` () `

Negation and Increment    --->    ` ! ` , ` ++ ` , ` -- `

Multiplication and Division    --->    ` * ` , ` / `

Addition and Subtraction    --->    ` + ` , ` - `

Relational    --->    ` < ` , ` <= ` , ` > ` , ` >= `

Equality    --->    ` == ` , ` != ` , ` === ` , ` !== `

AND    --->    ` && `

OR    --->    ` || `

Conditional    --->    ` ? : `

Assignment    --->    ` = ` , ` += ` , ` -= ` , ` *= `

# Comparison Operators 

_Comparison Operators will compare values and return a Boolean as an answer_


## Signals 

` = `       ----->       Assigning value   

` == `      ----->       Equal to

` != `      ----->       Diffent to

` === `     ----->       Strictly the same

` !== `     ----->       Strictly different

` > `       ----->       Greater than

` >= `      ----->       Greater than or equal to

` < `       ----->       Less than

` <= `      ----->       Less than or equal to
